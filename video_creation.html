Video Creation

Video Creations

My progress in video production is expanding as I continue to develop my skills in motion picture techniques. Click the titles!

"Break."
Break. A response to our ever evolving environment that continues technological progress while our social tendencies are on the high regression. When did we stop appreciating the finer things in life? The video is edited in Premier Pro and recorded on a R50 Camera.
"Simple."
Hopscotch turned into Crossy Road. Go Fish became online solitaire. Jump Rope became Flappy Bird. Games seemed more fun when we didn't have to tap for the fun to happen. Video Created in Premier Pro.
"World Domination."
A satirical take on the rising fear of AI world domination — because what better way to address existential dread than with a cartoon villain? This video was animated using Adobe Character Animator with a fully custom-designed character. Through humor, irony, and a slightly too-real monologue, it pokes fun at our anxieties around artificial intelligence, power, and the future we’re coding ourselves into.
"Finally Not Done."
The art of letting go: Celebrating what never needed an ending. An experiment with Blender.
