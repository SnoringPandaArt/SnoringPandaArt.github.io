Masked

A conversation between data that takes the shape of art and manifests into our tangible world.

This project explores how technology breathes life into objects while preserving the essence of traditional art. Using digital sculpting tools like Blender, I created a Japanese-inspired Oni mask to highlight the interplay between materiality and immateriality.
