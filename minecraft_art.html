Minecraft Art

Creative expressions through the world of blocks.

This section showcases digital media stepping "foot" into our 3D landscape. The inspiration for this piece was to showcase societal views on ADHD.

"My kid has an award for being top of his class." "My kid is going to law school." "What's wrong with mines?" Blah Blah Blah.

"Stop running around!" "Sit still!" "Why don't you get this? If you can't even get this problem right, how do you expect to get anywhere in life?"

Generational trauma mixed with denial can really mess a kid up. Sounds like a recipe for developmental struggles.
