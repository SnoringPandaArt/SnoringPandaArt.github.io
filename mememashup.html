"They're Eating Rex!"

2024. Sept 14

Satire Approach to Trump VS Harris Debate

"They're Eating Rex!"
Photoshop Demo. It seems that as the presidential debate continues, it's starting to feel more and more like a very bad comedy. Two individuals duking it out with political jargon thrown in the mix to stay relevant. Maybe we are oblivious people running around in circles and there are people out there eating your neighbors' pets. I'm sure pigs can fly one day.

Photoshop Demo. The main purpose of this assignment was to showcase modern and relevant ideas that describe and define our society while using photoshop and colliding the methods with memes because nothing keeps anything more relevant than tongue-and-cheek humor with a hint of satire.
