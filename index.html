About Me

My name is Brandon Vuong—an aspiring digital artist who dabbles in various mediums, because why limit myself, right? Art, for me, isn’t just about creating something pretty; it’s about making loud, bold statements that cut through the noise of the world, louder than the constant chaos we all carry inside.

This portfolio? It’s my slow, methodical descent into the abyss of creativity, where I try to make sense of a world that, let’s face it, rarely makes sense. But I’ll keep at it, using nothing but my hands to carve out my place.

Born and raised in Silicon Valley—where the rent’s absurd and true creativity seems like a rare commodity—I draw inspiration from artists like Shepard Fairey and Banksy. They’ve turned art into a weapon, taking on modern-day giants and their iron fists, armed with nothing more than a spray can and sheer defiance.

Art isn’t just my passion—it’s my revolt.
