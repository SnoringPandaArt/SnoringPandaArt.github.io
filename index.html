Now Why Am I Doing This And Why Should You Care?

Hi! My name is Brandon Vuong! I enjoy making art and I don't fear the unknown! I like trying different art styles, mediums, and techniques. For a while, I stuck only to what I knew, which happened to be sketching. Within this website, I will showcase my journey and hopefully inspire others to chase creativity and laugh in the face of uncertainty. Thanks for visiting!
