Biography of Dale Chihuly

Dale Chihuly was born on September 20, 1941 currently 83 yrs old, in Tacoma, Washington. He is an American artist noted for revolutionizing the studio glass movement and elevating blown glass from the realm of craft to fine art. He began his journey into the world of glassblowing during his studies at the University of Washington and later continued at the Rhode Island School of Design, where he established the glass program.

In 1976, Chihuly suffered a car accident that left him blind in one eye. Despite this, he continued to work and created some of his most famous works afterward. His contributions have included founding Pilchuck Glass School and producing monumental glass sculptures that have been installed in museums and gardens worldwide.
